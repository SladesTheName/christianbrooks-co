# ChristianBrooks.co

## Project
Personal brand website for Christian Brooks.
React app. Hosted on Vercel. Domain: christianbrooks.co.

## Brand voice
Honest, grounded, faith-driven. Sounds like a real person
talking to one person. No hype. No pressure. Not corporate.

## Design direction
Clean and minimal. Warm tones - think olive greens, cream,
soft gold accents. Calm authority, not polished salesperson.

## Who I am
Husband. Father. Follower of Jesus. IT manager building
my way out on purpose, not in desperation. Documenting the
real process. Haven't made it yet and not pretending I have.

## Site sections
1. Hero - who I am, one honest line
2. My story - real, not a resume
3. What I create - content about digital income and AI tools
4. Free resource - Escape Scorecard (low pressure, optional)
5. Brand inquiries - email contact for partnerships

## Tone rules
- No urgency language
- No scarcity tactics
- Speak to one person, not a crowd
- Faith is visible, not performative
